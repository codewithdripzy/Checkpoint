# 🔦 Concept: Checkpoint

> A decentralized proximity ecosystem bridging digital presence and physical interaction.

---

## 🛰️ Project Vision

Checkpoint is an open-source project designed to enable device tracking and contact discovery in a decentralized, offline-first world. By utilizing Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) and a mesh of low-power "Checkpointers," we create a resilient network for physical-digital interaction without relying on central internet infrastructure.

## 🏗️ Core Mechanisms

### 👤 Peer-to-Peer (P2P)

The P2P layer enables instantaneous information exchange when two Checkpoint users cross paths.
- **Contact Identification**: Securely identify known contacts in your vicinity using SHA-256 hashed phone numbers.
- **Offline Messaging**: (Planned feature) Exchange text-based alerts or messages over BLE.
- **Privacy-Forward**: Users are anonymous nodes unless a mutual contact match occurs or explicit permission is granted.

### 📍 Peer-to-Checkpoint (P2C)

Checkpointers are distributed hardware nodes serving as resilient anchors in the physical world.
- **Dynamic Pointers**: Nodes provide accurate GPS and local contextual data to any passing user.
- **Global Coverage**: A mesh of checkpointers can be deployed in any environment—urban or remote.
- **Real-Time Context**: Passing a checkpointer allows for instantaneous location updates and information fetching.

## 🛡️ Key Advantages

- **Resilient Recovery**: Unique kinetic-powered chips allow for tracking stolen or lost devices even without primary power.
- **Privacy-First Architectures**: End-to-end encryption and decentralized data models ensure user safety.
- **No Internet Dependency**: All primary features (discovery, matching, base location) operate via radio.

## 📱 The Prototype

### Mobile "Pointer" Chips
A proposed hardware layer for integration into mobile devices:
- **Kinetic Energy Harvesting**: Miniaturized kinetic collectors to charge a power backup during simple user movements.
- **Ultra-Fast Communication**: Optimized to exchange proximity headers in milliseconds.

### Solar Checkpointers
Environmental anchors:
- **Autonomous Power**: Solar-powered, weather-resistant capsules.
- **GPS-Assisted**: Real-time satellite data synchronization to ensure positioning accuracy.

## 📈 Future Applications

- **Lost & Found**: Advanced tracking and community-driven recovery.
- **Logistics**: Efficient, zero-infrastructure tracking for assets.
- **Travel & Safety**: Real-time position sharing with trusted contacts in remote areas.
